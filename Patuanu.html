PATUANU

Patuanu is a Hack'n'Slash 3D style game. Its history is totally oriented by the culture of the tribes Marajoara and Tupi. The game takes place in Brazil and the world is at the height of a war between two powerful gods: Girador, the creator of men and living beings and Anhangá, an evil being who seeks power corrupting the creations of his enemy.

Patuanu is in the middle of this war, as it is the main weapon of Girador, which to force her to fight at his side, curses her, preventing her from dying. To free herself from this curse and avenge the destruction of her tribe, she must gather forces from the four kingdoms of the creation of the world (animal, vegetable, mineral and spiritual) to face his greatest enemy, the God Anhangá.
