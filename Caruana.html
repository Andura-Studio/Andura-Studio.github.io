CARUANA

Caruana is the Endless Tower Defense for mobile that will be launched at the Google Play Store and Apple App Store by Andurá Studio.

The game takes place between the conflict of two gods: Girador, the creator of men and living beings, and Anhangá, who, incessantly, seeks power and tries to misrepresent Girador's creations. The goal is to protect Vila Marajoara from enemy invasions.
